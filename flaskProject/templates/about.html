Our Story

Brand Story

The Pet-Friendly Travel Project was born out of a deep understanding of the challenges faced by pet owners in their daily lives and during travel. As pet companionship becomes an integral part of modern life, we recognized the need for a comprehensive resource that empowers pet owners to explore the world with their furry friends without the stress of uncertainty.

Our story begins with countless tales of frustration and disappointment from pet owners like Xiaonan, who yearned to share everyday experiences and adventures with their pets but were often met with barriers. We saw a clear gap in the market—a lack of reliable and up-to-date information on pet-friendly establishments. This led to the vision of creating a platform that not only collects but also verifies and updates information on pet-friendly locations.

From the initial idea, our brand has grown to embody the spirit of community and collaboration. We believe in the power of shared experiences and the bond between pets and their owners. Our brand story is one of bringing people and their pets closer together, breaking down barriers, and fostering a more inclusive and pet-friendly world.

Through the Pet-Friendly Travel Project, we aim to build a community where every journey is a shared experience, and no pet owner has to leave their beloved companion behind. Our brand stands for trust, convenience, and the joy of exploring new places with your pet by your side.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To provide pet owners with a seamless and worry-free travel experience by offering the most comprehensive, up-to-date database of pet-friendly locations. Our mission is to ensure that every pet owner can easily find and access information about pet-friendly restaurants, hotels, shops, and public spaces, thereby enhancing the joy and convenience of traveling with pets.

Vision: Our vision is to create a world where traveling with pets is not just a possibility but a norm. We envision a future where pet owners can confidently explore new destinations, knowing that they have access to accurate and reliable information about pet-friendly amenities. We strive to foster a community that supports and encourages the inclusion of pets in everyday activities and travel, ultimately leading to a more pet-friendly society.
